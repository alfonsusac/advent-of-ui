# Christmas Countdown Timer

Santa wants a real-time countdown displayed in the workshop to keep everyone on track for the big day. This countdown will show how many days, hours, minutes, and seconds remain until Christmas.

## Challenge:

Design a dynamic countdown timer with festive styles. It should:

Update every second.
Include a holiday background, such as snowflakes or candy canes.
Adapt responsively for different screen sizes.
